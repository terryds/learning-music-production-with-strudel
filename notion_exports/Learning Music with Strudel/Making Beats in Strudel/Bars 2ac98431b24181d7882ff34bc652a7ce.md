# Bars

So far, all of the drum patterns you've looked at loop back to the beginning after four beats. This grouping of four beats is called a *bar* or *measure*.

In Strudel, we call it *a cycle*

Make sure you’ve read https://strudel.cc/understand/cycles/ before continuing.

Here's a drum pattern that's two bars long:

[Bars | Learning Music](https://learningmusic.ableton.com/make-beats/bars.html)

![image.png](Bars/image.png)

You can try writing it in Strudel using `arrange`  and `stack` .

Basically these drum patterns are an arrangement of 1 cycle of the first bar, and then another cycle of the second bar.
